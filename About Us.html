About Us

Where it all began

In 2022, Precious Radebe founded Empowering the Nation, inspired by the untold stories of her parents and relatives who never had the opportunity to pursue formal education. Their resilience planted the seed for a vision to uplift domestic workers and gardeners through accessible and practical training.

Mission

We are more than just a training provider , we are a movement dedicated to:

  • Community upliftment
  • Small business development
  • Lifelong learning
  • Inclusive and Safe learning spaces

Vision

Our vision is to empower individuals with the skills, confidence, and opportunities to create a better future for themselves and their families. We aspire to build a South Africa where every person — regardless of background — has access to quality education, practical training, and the opportunity to uplift their community through lifelong learning and empowerment.

With centres in Sandton, Soweto, and Midrand, we bring education closer to the communities.

Courses Offered

At Empowering the Nation, our six-month and six-week short courses and programmes are carefully designed to equip learners with practical, real-world skills that promote personal and professional growth.

We offer a diverse range of courses, including:

  • First Aid
  • Sewing
  • Cooking & Nutrition
  • Landscaping & Gardening
  • Life Skills Development
  • Child Minding and Caregiving
